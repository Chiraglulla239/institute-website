Prof. MOHIT KUKREJA

Teaching Engineering has always been more of experience to me than a profession. Subjects like Engineering Mechanics and Engineering Graphics, which are usually considered to be most difficult, come naturally to me. On the top of that, when a student can relate most of the subjects with the things that they actually use in real world, that feeling itself is exemplary.
Professional Education is still in nascent stage in India, where most subjects are taught in theoretical manner. I wish to change that in a drastic way, where a class should be considered as a place for students' ideas and not some place where concepts are bombarded upon students for getting good marks in Exams.
